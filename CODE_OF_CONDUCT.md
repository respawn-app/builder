# Code of Conduct

Builder is a professional technical project. Participation in this repository is expected to stay respectful, constructive, and focused on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Discuss ideas, code, and tradeoffs in good faith.
- Keep feedback technical, specific, and respectful.
- Stay on topic in issues, pull requests, and discussions.
- Accept maintainer decisions about scope, direction, and moderation.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, intimidation, or stalking
- Personal attacks, insults, or abusive language
- Hate speech or discriminatory abuse
- Doxxing or sharing private information without permission
- Deliberate disruption, trolling, or spam
- Repeated bad-faith arguing after a decision has been made

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, lock conversations, reject contributions, or ban participants from project spaces when behavior violates this policy.

## Reporting

Report serious issues to `hello@respawn.pro`.

## Scope

This policy applies to this repository and its official project spaces.
